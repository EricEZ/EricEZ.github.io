Teamwork means everything in this fast-paced multiplayer game!

A laser tethers you and your teammate together, requiring you to work together in order to get the laser to slice away the competition. First team to touch an opponent with their laser is the victor! Developed by 25PercentGames, I was the level designer for this project as well as the music producer.
